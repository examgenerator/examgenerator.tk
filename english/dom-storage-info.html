Why do we use cookies?

Actually, we don't use cookies, but web storage. Web storage is a technology which allows web sites to store some data to browser. It's similar to cookies, but supperior in some ways. Read more at Wikipedia.

So, why is there cookies warning?

According to the EU, "a cookie is a small piece of data that a website asks your browser to store on your computer or mobile device." So, it sounds like it's considered as cookies by the EU.
