About Exam Generator

Exam Generator is a simple web app. All teacher will find it very useful while preparing exams. Imagine you have thought of 100 questions about some topic, or you have given a list of 150 foreign words to your students, or you have 60 terms to explain. Now you are preparing an exam. Which questions are the best? With Exam Generator you don't have to worry about that. Just tell Exam Generator questions you've prepared and it will generate your exam.
